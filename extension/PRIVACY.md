# Privacy Policy — Metriq Prompt Optimizer

_Last updated: 2026-07-13_

Metriq Prompt Optimizer is a browser extension that rewrites the prompt you are
drafting in an AI chatbot into a more focused one.

## What it accesses

- **The text of the prompt you are currently typing**, and only when you click
  the extension's ✦ button. It reads that text from the page's input box to
  analyze and rewrite it.
- The extension requests access to all sites so it can **detect** whether a page
  is an AI chatbot. On any page that is not an AI chat, it does nothing.

## What it does NOT do

- **No data is collected, stored, or transmitted anywhere.** All analysis runs
  locally inside your browser tab. Your prompt is never sent to Metriq, to any
  server, or to any third party.
- No account, no login, no tracking, no analytics, no cookies.
- It does not read page content other than the prompt input, and only when you
  explicitly click the ✦ button.

## Data sharing

None. The extension makes no network requests.
